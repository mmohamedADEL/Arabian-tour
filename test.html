Grieving the Gaza Granddad Who Wept for the ‘Soul of His Soul’

Khaled Nabhan grieved peacefully for his granddaughter Reem last year. On Monday, Israel killed him in Gaza, too.

His tragic death highlights the enduring cycle of grief and loss experienced by countless families in Gaza. Khaled, a symbol of resilience, is remembered for his love and dedication to his family, even in the face of unimaginable hardship.
